Significance of Password Strength

Author: Kim, E., Miller, T., & Puig, A. (2024, October 11). Creating Strong Passwords and Other Ways To Protect Your Accounts. Federal Trade Commission Consumer Advice.
Abstract: The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) offers a variety of resources to help consumers make informed decisions and protect themselves from fraud and scams. The Trade Commission is responsible for fair business practices. It also assists in protecting consumers from becoming victims. The commission accepts consumer complaints and assesses potential fraud and unfair practices. It also provides information to the public on how to protect from identity theft, safety in online shopping, and scam alerts for consumers. The Federal Trade Commission also offers resources for privacy and security in an effort to protect the general public from cybercrimes.
Author: Anderson, M., & Choong, Y.-Y. (2023, October 10). Cybersecurity Awareness Month 2023 Blog Series: Using Strong Passwords and a Password Manager. NIST.
Abstract: The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is a leader in protecting the development and productive use of information technology. The NITL works to develop and enforce guidelines that protect the security and privacy of federal information systems. The NITL develops protocols related to digital authentication and electronic credentials. They work to create outreach efforts and activities with organizations, government, and educational organizations.
